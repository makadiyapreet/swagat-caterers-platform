From Kitchen Dreams to Reality

It started with one simple goal: To serve food that tastes like home, but looks like a celebration.

Swagat Caterers was founded in 2014. What began as a small service for family gatherings has grown into a premier catering company serving over 200+ events a year.

We believe that "Food is the soul of any event." That's why we grind our own spices, source local produce, and never compromise on hygiene.
